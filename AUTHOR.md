## Dynamic Dataset Synchronization Framework (DDSF)

### Concept Author

Mohd Anas

### Date of First Public Publication

20 June 2026

### Statement

I, Mohd Anas, publicly document the concept known as the Dynamic Dataset Synchronization Framework (DDSF).

DDSF is a conceptual architecture intended to address dataset staleness in deployed AI applications through centralized dataset management and synchronization mechanisms.

This repository serves as a public record of the concept, its documentation, architecture, and future development.
